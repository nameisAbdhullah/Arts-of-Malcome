About Malcolm

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on the stunning North West coast of Scotland. With over a decade of experience, Malcolm has developed a passion for capturing the untamed beauty of the Scottish landscape, its rich wildlife, and coastal birds. His work is driven by a deep love for nature and storytelling through imagery.

Beyond his landscape and wildlife photography, Malcolm is highly sought after for his ability to capture the essence of special occasions. Whether it's weddings, portraits, or other celebrations, he brings his expertise, creativity, and attention to detail to every project.
